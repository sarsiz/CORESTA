Prime Minister of India, Mr Narendra Modi started the campaign “Digital India” in the year 2014. So inspiring from this campaign we have made a web based application called Coresta.
Coresta means Complete Record of Students Attendance. It provides various functionalities like managing the number of students, adding and deleting students from a class, seeing their records.  Teachers don't need to maintain the attendance register as with Coresta you can take and view attendance of the whole class. Teacher can also send the warning mails to students who are short of attendance and can view their records.
No matter where the teachers are they can view and monitor the performance of their class from anywhere.
So the motive of developing this project is to make our college digital, minimizing the use of paper and thus saving environment.
